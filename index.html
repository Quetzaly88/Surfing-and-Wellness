Surf practices for the well being

Surfing as many other sports is proven to enhance our mental and physical health. Studies has shown that the contact with nature and the adrenaline promote a liberation of negative emotions.

Get inspired

In this world where money is an important asset for living we forget to be playfull and creative. Surfing mixed with yoga and surfskate gives you and alternative to the traditional sports. Here, you have the opportunity to become a child again, make misstakes and stimulate your senses.

This program is designed for people in need of feeling better after struggling with chronic pain, stress, depression and phisical disabilities. We invite you to check some of the ispiration sites to realize how benefitial this is for your health.
