Surf for well being

There are many health benefits with surfing, both physically and mentally. It's great for your body and can even help you maintain a balanced mental state and a positive attitude towards life.

Be playfull!

In this world where everybody is busy, we forget to be playfull and creative. Our programs with surfing, yoga and surfskate gives you an alternative to do something new. Here, you have the opportunity to become a child again, make misstakes and stimulate your senses. Additionally you are enjoying the outdoors, the beach, meeting new people and having fun with new friends. It’s an exciting, fun and challenging sport that will add value to your life.

Programs focused on your health

This program is designed to make you feel better. We have worked with people who has suffered with chronic pain, stress, depression and phisical disabilities. There is no better way to relax your body and soul than to go for a surf, have a yoga lesson, take a walk in the forest or just be outdoors.

Resurface

Surfing has become medicine for military veterans suffering from physical and mental trauma. Resurface follows veterans who find that the ocean is the one place they can go to for peace.
